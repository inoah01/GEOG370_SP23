My Map

This is a map of a subsection of Oakland County, MI. In particular, this map is a subsection of the city in Oakland County
that I am from, Auburn Hills. I used 2 vector layers in addition to OpenStreetMap, those layers were Oakland County school districts
and Michigan cities & townships.

All data used for this project:

  • Oakland County school districts
  • Michigan Cities & Townships
