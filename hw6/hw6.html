Homework 6: Michigan Census Data Choropleth

Below, is my choropleth map of Michigan counties and Census data. More specifically, this
map looks at the median household income (in 2021-adjusted USD) for families across Michigan counties.
The U.S. Census defines a family as, ". . . a group of two people or more (one of whom is the householder)
related by birth, marriage, or adoption and residing together . . .". There was not any particular reasoning
behind me choosing to look at income and families aside from the fact that I thought those would be
broad enough to show change across Michigan. There was, however, a particular reason in me choosing
symbology that I chose. Green is a color that is commonly associated with nature or money. In this
case, it is not variation in nature that is being studied, but rather income (money).
