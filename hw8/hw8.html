More about the interactive map:

  • The project abstract can be viewed by hovering over the "i" in the upper-right-hand corner
  • Layers can be toggled using the checkboxes directly below the abstract box
  • Click on a county or individual EV charging station to see more data
  • The data sources used for this project can be found at the bottom of the map abstract
    or at the bottom of the page
  • Use the ruler tool in the upper-right-hand corner to measure between a distance between two points (metric units)
  • To view the map in a new tab, click "map:" (above)

Data used for this project:

  • Cleaned point layer of NC EV charging stations
  • 5 Mile clipped and dissolved buffer of EV charging stations
  • HW7 Choropleth as a shapefile layer
